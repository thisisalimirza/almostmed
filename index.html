Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with AlmostMed

How does the mentor matching process work?

Our AI-powered system analyzes your academic background, target schools, and career goals to match you with current medical students who have successfully navigated a similar path. We ensure compatibility in areas like academic interests, cultural fit, and communication style.

What's included in the mentorship program?

You'll receive personalized guidance on MCAT preparation, application strategy, personal statement review, interview preparation, and ongoing support throughout your pre-med journey. Our mentors provide actionable feedback and share their firsthand experiences.

How often will I meet with my mentor?

Meeting frequency is flexible and based on your needs. Most students meet with their mentors bi-weekly for 1-hour sessions, with additional support via messaging. During critical periods like application deadlines, you can schedule more frequent sessions.
